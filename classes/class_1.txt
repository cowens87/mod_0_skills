Class: Event Rooms

Attributes:
room_name (string)
current_room_capacity (integer)
drinks_included (boolean)
food_menu_options (array)
total_room_capacity (integer)

Methods:
change_room (will change the room_name)
available_capacity (total_room_capacity-current_room_capacity)
include_drinks (will toggle drinks_included to true)
add_food (will add food items into the food_menu_options array)
